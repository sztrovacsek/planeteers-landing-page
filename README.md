# Planeteers
Planeteers landing page

## What is Planeteers

Planeteers is a discussion-based board game on sustainability where children explore the balance between cost, health, and the environment. There are no right or wrong answers, just choices, trade-offs, and conversations. Through play even complex global challenges become tangible and relatable and surprisingly intuitive.
