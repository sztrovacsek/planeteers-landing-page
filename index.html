How it works

No right answers.
Just real conversations.

Planeteers puts children at the center of meaningful choices — the same kinds of choices our planet faces every day. The discussion it sparks is the lesson.

🎲

Play-based learning

Children explore trade-offs between cost, health, and the environment through guided gameplay. Complex global challenges become tangible and surprisingly intuitive.

💬

Discussion-first design

Every round opens a conversation. There are no wrong moves — just choices, consequences, and the chance to think differently. Critical thinking emerges naturally.

🧩

Modular & adaptable

Designed for different cultures, languages, and curricula. Expansion packs deepen the experience. One game, endless contexts.

For teachers

Built for classrooms. Loved by educators.

Teachers told us they needed better tools to truly engage students. Planeteers gives them a ready-to-run session that amazes even the most skeptical teacher.

  • 📦 Classroom kit: Everything a teacher needs in one box — no preparation required beyond reading a one-pager.
  • 🌐 Culturally adaptable: Tested across India and Switzerland. Scenarios and language can be localised for any market.
  • 📚 Curriculum-aligned: Integrates with existing sustainability, ethics, and social studies lessons — not a disruption, an enhancement.
  • 🔄 Expansion packs: New scenarios keep the game fresh across school years. Partner with educational publishers for deeper integration.
